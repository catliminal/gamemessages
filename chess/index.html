Chess Messages

Challenge your friends to chess!

Play chess with your friends directly in iMessage!

Just choose between playing as white or black to begin.

Chess Messages lets you challenge your friends to a game of chess. You can play a different game with each of your friends, even in the same chat thread! Everyone in a group chat will be able to watch as spectators to games they're not participating in.

Unlock a variety of beautiful board and piece styles.

Play a leisurely infinite game or unlock timed games for extra excitement. Highlighted board squares will automatically show you legal moves after tapping a piece. When you beat your opponent you can capture the moment to your camera roll!
